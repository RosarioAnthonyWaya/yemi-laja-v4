Full Biography

Yemi Laja

"I create music, stories, and platforms that inspire faith, culture, and global impact."

Yemi Laja is a London-based worship artist, creative director, life coach, and the founder of Mastercraft Global Limited. With over 13 years of experience spanning music, spoken word, stage acting, film, and creative direction, Yemi has built a career rooted in purpose and shaped by excellence.

Her journey began in the pews of a local church — a young woman with a voice and a vision. That calling took her from intimate worship gatherings to main stages across the UK and beyond, from producing independent music to directing full-scale creative productions for organisations, churches, and media outlets.

In founding Mastercraft Global Limited, Yemi created a platform not just for her own work, but for other creatives. The company houses coaching programmes, design services, and the Global Talent Endorsement Programme — all aimed at discovering and elevating gifted individuals who might otherwise go unnoticed.

Whether she's leading worship at a 2,000-seat conference, coaching an emerging artist through a creative breakthrough, or directing a brand identity for a corporate client — Yemi brings the same quality: presence, precision, and passion.

Career Highlights

The Journey

2010
The Beginning — First Worship Album

Released her debut worship project, stepping into the public arena as a worship artist and establishing her distinct sound — soulful, spirit-led, and culturally rooted.

2014
Stage & Spoken Word — Theatre Performances

Expanded beyond music into stage performance and spoken word, appearing in multiple theatre productions across London and delivering spoken word pieces at national events.

2017
Founded Mastercraft Global Limited

Launched the creative and production company to provide a formal structure for her growing portfolio — housing design services, productions, and talent development under one roof.

2020
Global Talent Endorsement Programme — Launched

Created one of her most impactful initiatives: a structured programme to identify, endorse, and connect emerging creatives with global platforms and opportunities.

2025
Mastercraft: The EP · Worship in Chaos Event

Released her most acclaimed EP to date and announced "Worship in Chaos" — a landmark live event bringing together worship, community, and cultural engagement in one night.

Values & Vision

What Drives
Everything

Every song, every stage, every programme — all of it is rooted in a set of convictions that Yemi holds close. These aren't corporate values. They're personal ones.

01
Excellence is a spiritual act.

Doing things well isn't about ego — it's about honour. Yemi believes every creative act is an offering, and offerings deserve the best.

02
Platforms exist to lift others.

Any platform Yemi builds is ultimately for the purpose of elevating others — not as a stage for herself, but as a launchpad for emerging voices.

03
Faith and culture are not opposites.

Yemi lives at the intersection of faith and creative culture — believing the two strengthen, not diminish, each other when brought together well.
