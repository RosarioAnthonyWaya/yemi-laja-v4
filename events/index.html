About the Event

When the World
Gets Loud

Worship in Chaos is Yemi Laja's signature live worship experience — a gathering designed for a world that rarely sits still. In a culture of noise, distraction, and relentless pace, this event creates a deliberate space for worship, encounter, and transformation.

This is more than a concert. It is a movement. Expect live original worship, spoken word, community moments, and an atmosphere where the sacred meets the cultural in a way few experiences do.

Whether you're a seasoned worshipper or someone who simply needs a moment of stillness — Worship in Chaos is for you.
