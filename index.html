About Yemi

Worship Artist.
Creative Leader.
Platform Builder.

"I don't just create — I build spaces where faith meets culture, and talent finds its voice."

Yemi Laja is a worship artist, creative director, life coach, and the founder of Mastercraft Global Limited. For over 13 years, she has moved between stages and boardrooms — leading worship, directing creative productions, coaching emerging talent, and building programmes that elevate artists globally.

Based in London, her work spans music, spoken word, film, and stage — all anchored in a deep sense of calling and a commitment to excellence.
