Spoken Word & Stage

Words That
Move People

"Every word is a seed. Every stage is a field. I plant deliberately."

Before the music was the word. Yemi's spoken word performances carry the same weight and intentionality as her worship — drawing from lived experience, faith, and cultural observation to deliver pieces that linger long after the room goes quiet.

Alongside her spoken word work, Yemi has appeared in stage productions as a performer and actress — bringing the same depth to dramatic storytelling that she brings to ministry.
